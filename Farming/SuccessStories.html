Success Stories

Our site has benefited many people, and these are some of the statements of people who have benefited from our site:



Hello, I am Ibrahim. I had many plants in my house, but most of these plants do not live long. Then I was looking for a way to preserve the plants and I found this site. I started following the instructions written on the site and I benefited a lot. There are plants and species on this site that did not live long. After I followed the methods of how to preserve them, they lived for many months. Thank you very much for creating this wonderful and beautiful site.



Hi, I am Omar. My father had a beautiful garden and he used to take care of his plants in the best possible way. A short time ago, my father passed away. I used to look at his garden and remember him every time. I made a promise to myself that I would take care of the garden for my father. At first, the situation was difficult and I started looking for ways to make it easier for me to take care of the plants. I found this site and I really liked the way it was coordinated, the view of the site and its beauty. Many thanks to those who created this site. Now I can take care of my father’s garden in the best possible way.



Howdy, My name is Ali and I am a student at school. One day, the teacher asked us to take care of a plant for a week. She gave each of us a different type of plant. On the first day, I did not know how to take care of the plant, so I decided to search for the topic and found your site. I found the same plant that I have and applied all the advice there. After a week, I gave it to the teacher and she praised me and told me that I am one of the few students who took care of the plant in this way. In short, I really thank you. You helped me and now I feel that I care more about plants.

Thank you to all the people who shared their experiences and love for our site. We really appreciate your words and hope you will be with us in our future work.
