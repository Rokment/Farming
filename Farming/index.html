Farming

Agriculture has merit and importance ,as the Messenger of God, peace be upon him,

says: (ما من مسلمٍ يغرس غرسًا، أو يزرع زرعًا، فيأكل منه طيرٌ أو إنسانٌ أو بهيمةٌ إلا كان له صدقةٌ).


We benefit from this hadith by highlighting the importance of agriculture and taking care of plants. That's why we designed this site to guide you on how to grow and care for your indoor plants.
